Frontier Firm Framework

Where does {{CUSTOMER_NAME}} sit on the journey?

Microsoft's Frontier Firm research maps organisations across three patterns of human-AI collaboration. Moving from P1 to P3 isn't about deploying more technology — it requires new norms: when to delegate, how to review agent output, what good enough looks like from a machine colleague. Frontier Firms see 3x higher ROI and 71% of employees say their company is thriving vs 39% globally.
P1
Human with Assistant

AI helps individuals. Humans drive.

AI tools assist individual employees in completing tasks faster. Each person works with AI independently — but there is no team-level orchestration and no delegated execution.

{{ORG_FOUNDATION_COUNT}} of {{ORG_COUNT}} orgs at this pattern
P2
Human-Agent Teams

Humans orchestrate. Agents execute.

Employees become "agent bosses" — they set goals, agents handle execution, humans review outputs. Tasks are delegated, not just assisted.

{{ORG_EXPANSION_COUNT}} of {{ORG_COUNT}} orgs at this pattern
P3
Agent-Operated

Agents handle whole workflows.

Agents operate autonomously across entire workflows with minimal human prompting. Humans focus on exceptions, governance, and outcomes.
