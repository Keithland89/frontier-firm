Frontier Firm Framework

What is a Frontier Firm?

A Frontier Firm is an organisation where humans and AI agents work together as teams on live workflows — not just individuals using AI as a personal tool. Microsoft defines three phases of this transformation.

Click each phase card to explore what it looks like in practice.

1
Pattern 1 · Foundation
Human with Assistant

Copilot augments individual work. Employees use AI to draft, summarise and research — but it's a personal tool, not yet part of shared team workflows.

Explore Pattern 1 →
Pattern 1 · Foundation
What this looks like in practice
Copilot is deployed but usage varies widely — heavy users sit alongside those who rarely open it
AI helps with individual tasks — drafting emails, summarising meetings, one-off searches
No consistent team-level routines or delegated workflows yet
Agents exist but are mostly personal or unused beyond their creator

Pattern 2 · Expansion
Human-Agent Teams

Habits are forming but not yet consistent across the full workforce

3
Pattern 3 · Frontier
Human-Led, Agent-Operated

AI agents run enterprise workflows end-to-end. Humans set direction and govern outcomes — agents execute. AI is embedded in how the organisation operates, not just how individuals work.

The Signals

What it takes to move an organisation to the Frontier

Frontier Firm progress isn’t measured in licenses or logins. It’s measured in behaviour — across every person, team, and function. Three observable signals tell the story.

Spread it across the org
Reach

A Frontier Firm is not defined by a few power users — it’s defined by AI becoming a capability everyone relies on. This signal measures whether AI reaches across roles, teams, and functions.

Turn use into routine
Habit

Occasional use doesn’t transform an organisation. Frontier Firms build AI into the daily rhythm of work. This signal measures whether people are converting from experimenters into habitual users.

Push past the basics
Skill

Early AI use is narrow and assistive. Advancing organisations move into sophisticated territory — directing agents, building multi-step workflows, using AI as a collaborator, not just a shortcut.
