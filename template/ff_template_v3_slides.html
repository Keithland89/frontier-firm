Frontier Firm Patterns

Three patterns of human-AI teaming

Pattern 1
Human with Assistant

Every employee has an AI assistant that helps them work better and faster. AI supports individual work: drafting, summarising, researching — in the flow of work.

Pattern 2
Human-Agent Teams

Agents join teams as digital colleagues, taking on specific tasks at human direction. Humans delegate; agents execute; humans steer and review.

Pattern 3
Agent-Operated

Humans set direction; agents execute business processes end-to-end. Substantial workflows run autonomously with oversight and governance.

These patterns coexist across different teams — this is expected and legitimate.
